About Us

Side Control is a vibrant Manhattan-based quartet carving their own space in the indie music scene. We fuse the energy of pop-punk with the improvisational spirit of jam bands and the melodic sensibilities of alternative rock, creating a sound that's both familiar and uniquely our own.


The band features the dynamic interplay of Evan Wolf (guitar/vocals) and Gaston Alvaro (guitar/vocals), the captivating vocals of Lindsay Voigt, the driving rhythms of Justin Duong on drums, and the versatile musicianship of Brent Greenlee on bass and keys.


Since forming, Side Control has been actively performing across the New York City area, bringing our energetic live shows to venues throughout Manhattan. We've also brought our music to audiences along the Jersey Shore and in Philadelphia, building a dedicated following with each performance.


As a new wave indie band with a passion for creating and sharing our music, Side Control is looking forward to expanding our footprint in the New York City music scene and beyond. Stay tuned for new music and upcoming shows!
